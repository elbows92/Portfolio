Moon_Tile

Who - personal project

What - industrial design / data visualization

Why - explore

When - may 2014

This project was an exercise in communicating complex systems and events through nonverbal, visual means. The chosen subject of the project was the Moon.

The final product was a 3D printed tile derived from an abstract graphic mark.
